ABOUT

Variety Quality Tasty

The Wendy's Foods was started by a PSG Techian as a small start-up in 2018 in Peelamedu.The main motto of our foods is "Variety Quality Tasty". To achieve this goal we will be working to our fullest. We are also planning to make our take away restaurant to a complete dining one and we need all your support in achieving this goal.The initial responses to our foods were amazing and now we are having 3 branches for our foods at the hotspot areas of Coimbatore to provide the best service to our customers.The customers can order their foods online and can take it from our restaurant,so that they don't need to wait while the food is being prepared.
